## 1. What is the Lead Diagnostic System?

### 1.1 Purpose

The Lead Diagnostic System **automatically qualifies and nurtures leads** who submit our career assessment form. It's like having an AI career consultant that works 24/7, analyzing every lead's profile and giving them a personalized roadmap + product recommendation.

**What it replaces:**
- ‚ùå Manual lead review (2-3 hours per lead)
- ‚ùå Generic email templates
- ‚ùå Guesswork about which product to offer
- ‚ùå Subjective "gut feel" qualification

**What it delivers:**
- ‚úÖ Instant personalized 2,500-word report (in 30 seconds)
- ‚úÖ Objective 0-100 readiness score
- ‚úÖ AI-recommended product based on profile + budget
- ‚úÖ Actionable next steps (30-day, 90-day, 6-month plans)

### 1.2 Business Impact

**For Marketing:**
- Track which campaigns bring qualified leads (vs just volume)
- See which lead sources have highest readiness scores
- Optimize ad spend based on score distribution by channel

**For Sales:**
- Know which leads to prioritize (hot vs warm vs cold)
- Understand lead's profile before first call
- Use report data to personalize pitch
- Reduce time spent qualifying unready leads

## 10. Best Practices & Pro Tips

### 10.1 Marketing Best Practices

**‚úÖ DO:**
- Use UTM on EVERY link
- Track avg score by campaign
- Retarget report viewers who didn't convert
- Create lookalikes of HIGH_TICKET leads

**‚ùå DON'T:**
- Promise "guaranteed visa"
- Use fear-based marketing
- Ignore low-score leads (future customers)

### 10.2 Sales Best Practices

**‚úÖ DO:**
- Review report BEFORE calling
- Prioritize HOT leads (call in 24-72h)
- Use lead's exact words from form
- Offer appropriate tier product

**‚ùå DON'T:**
- Call COLD leads immediately
- Ignore stated budget
- Promise unrealistic timelines
- Discount heavily on first call

### 10.3 Pro Tips

**Tip 1:** Use "score improvement" as hook ‚Üí "Let's increase your 54 to 70+ in 3 months"
**Tip 2:** Anchor to lifetime value ‚Üí "R$4,500 investment = 4 days of US salary"
**Tip 3:** Create urgency ‚Üí "Next cohort starts March 1, only 3 spots left"
**Tip 4:** Upsell after initial win ‚Üí Sell ebook first, then session, then mentorship
**Tip 5:** Social proof from similar profiles ‚Üí "Rafael had score 56, now at Microsoft Seattle"
