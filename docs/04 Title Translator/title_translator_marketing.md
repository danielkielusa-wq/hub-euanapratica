## What Title Translator Does

Title Translator (branded as "Experience Translator" in the UI) is an AI-powered tool that solves a critical pain point for Brazilian professionals applying to US jobs: **job title mismatch**.

In Brazil, a "Coordenador de TI" might manage 5 developers and handle technical architecture. In the USA, that same role could be called "IT Team Lead," "Engineering Manager," or "Technical Lead" depending on company culture and seniority norms. Using the wrong title on a resume or LinkedIn profile means recruiters won't find you, and when they do, they'll misunderstand your level of responsibility.

Title Translator analyzes your Brazilian title, work responsibilities, and experience level, then gives you three ranked US title alternatives with:
- **Confidence scores** (how well this title matches your profile)
- **Salary ranges** in USD (what you should expect to earn)
- **Example companies** that use this exact title
- **Sample job description snippets** (so you can see how it's used in real postings)

It tells you which title to use and explains why it's the best fit for your specific situation.
